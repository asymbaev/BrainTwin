6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Your data is encrypted when stored in our database
  • Secure Authentication: Receipt-based authentication system (no passwords to compromise)
  • Access Controls: Row-level security policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Payment Security: Apple handles all payment processing (PCI DSS compliant); we never see your credit card information
  • API Security: JWT verification on all backend functions
  • Infrastructure Security: SOC 2 compliant cloud infrastructure (Supabase)

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
